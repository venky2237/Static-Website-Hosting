Top National Parks in India

Ranthambore National Park

Location: Rajasthan

Famous for Bengal tigers and historical ruins inside the forest.

Kaziranga National Park

Location: Assam

Home to the world's largest population of one-horned rhinoceroses.

Sundarbans National Park

Location: West Bengal

Known for mangrove forests and the elusive Royal Bengal Tiger.

Gir Forest National Park

Location: Gujarat

The only natural habitat of the Asiatic lion in the world.

Periyar National Park

Location: Kerala

Known for elephants and scenic boat cruises on Periyar Lake.
